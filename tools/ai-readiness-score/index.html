Get a 0-100 score showing how well AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews can discover, understand, and cite your website.

What is AI Readiness?

AI Readiness measures how effectively your website can be discovered, understood, and cited by generative AI search engines. Unlike traditional SEO, AI readiness evaluates your site across 12 dimensions including AI crawlability, structured data, citation readiness, and agent-friendliness.

A high AI Readiness score means your site is more likely to appear in AI-generated answers, be cited by ChatGPT and Perplexity, and show up in Google AI Overviews.
