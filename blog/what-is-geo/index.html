What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?

Published July 2025 · 8 min read

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing your website so that AI-powered search engines — like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Claude — can discover, understand, and cite your content.

If traditional SEO is about ranking #1 on Google, GEO is about being the source AI engines quote when users ask questions.

Why GEO Matters Now

AI search is exploding. By 2025, over 1 billion people use AI search engines monthly. Perplexity alone handles 100M+ queries per day. Google now shows AI Overviews for a large share of searches.

If your site isn't optimized for AI discovery, you're invisible to this growing audience. GEO ensures your content is crawlable, understandable, and citable by AI models.

12 Dimensions of GEO Readiness

  1. AI Crawlability — Is your robots.txt allowing AI crawlers?
  2. AI Guidance — Do you have an llms.txt file?
  3. Structured Data — Is your content marked up with schema.org?
  4. Meta & Social Tags — Are your meta tags complete?
  5. Content Quality — Is your content substantial and well-structured?
  6. E-E-A-T Signals — Do you show Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust?
  7. Brand & Entity — Is your brand recognizable to AI?
  8. Citation Readiness — Can AI easily cite your content?
  9. Discovery Endpoints — Do you have AI-specific endpoints?
  10. Agent-Friendliness — Can AI agents interact with your site?
  11. Freshness — Is your content up to date?
  12. Negative Signals — Any factors that hurt AI visibility?

How to Get Started

  1. 1. Run a free GEO audit to get your baseline score
  2. 2. Check your robots.txt isn't blocking AI crawlers
  3. 3. Create an llms.txt file for your site
  4. 4. Add structured data (JSON-LD) to key pages
  5. 5. Improve content with citations, stats, and clear structure
  6. 6. Re-audit monthly to track your score
